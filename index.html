Astronomy is the study that enables us to more understand the universe. It has different branches: stars, planets, black holes, cosmology, galaxies. The most important tools in astronomy are telescopes. Through them, we can observe the different objects in the universe. Light coming from the universe is collected via these telescopes. The light is then processed and analyzed to get the relevant information. Astronomy is a very ancient science. Civilisations have used astronomy for different purposes: navigation, time for harvesting, priars for Islamic society,... To many of us, astronomy can answer our most difficult questions: Are we alone? How the universe was born? Is the universe infinite?... and this is what drives many research in this field
Despite the great development in Science and instrumentation, our knowledge of the universe is still very limited. First our lifetime is so short compared to the lifetime of the universe(Billions of years), there are many objects that vary in timescales of million of years. For example the merging of 2 nearby galaxies. So all the observations that have been done during the last years is not enough to understand how stuff go there.
